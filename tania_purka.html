Purka

American dancer, of African descent. She was born in Los Angeles. Engaged in dancing since 6 years, the experience in hip hop more than 9 years. Participant and winner of major dance competitions and venues: Just Debout, WOD (World of Dance), Just Dance, Dance Championship, Australia Hip Hop and more.

Newcomers begin to learn basic movements, train the body. During workouts, light ligaments are given to the inclusion of muscle memory, which is memorized little by bit. To feel freedom of movement, will take from a couple of months to half a year. At the end of the lessons, in the hall are conducted battles and freestyle sessions, where everyone shows what he is capable of. As a bonus in each group will be selected and awarded the best dancers, after each month of training. Qualified Itancor Mari will teach and support you everything.
