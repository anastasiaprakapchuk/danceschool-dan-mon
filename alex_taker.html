American dancer born in California. Manur loved to dance from early childhood. From the age of eight he danced breakdance, and after that he began hip-hop. But most of all he fell in love with the style of the C.R.U.M.P dance. After a long education and victories in the World Dance Championships, Manur became one of the best teachers in the world.

This Sharp, aggressive, impulsive dance requires the performer to have excellent command of his body and emotions. It is necessary to know the basic elements, as well as certain gestures inherent in this style of C.R.U.M.P which we would gladly teach you. All movements should be understandable, saturated, fast and clear.
