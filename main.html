locking

Locking is focused on spectacular performance and constant interaction with viewers. On the one hand it is based on Klounade, but on the other it is an independent direction. All movements are actually very simple, anyone can do them, but they must be present spirit Lokinga. For Lokinga, as well as for hip-hop, from which were borrowed many basic elements, are characteristic of sharpmovements, various dance elements with swaying hands and a lot of various jumps.

popping

Popping is the style of street dance, which is based on the technique of fast stress and muscle relaxation, so a great physical form you are provided. In addition, like other street dances, popping is quite often used in battles, designed to prove its advantage over other dancers in public. This will surely help you liberated and gain self-confidence. The name "popping" dates back to the seventies of the last century. According to legend, Sam Solomon, founder of "Electronic Boogaloo lockers", at each contraction of muscles shouted the word "pop", thus giving the name of a new kind of dance.

To live in a big city and to remain outside energetic dance simply it is impossible, it has long become a subculture: it both music, and plastic, and hobby of graffiti. The technique is based on special jumps and turns, accented movements of the head, arms and legs.
